# 10x Thinking
#claudeai

## The Framework

Don't ask "How can we improve by 10%?"
Ask **"How can we improve by 10x?"**

10% improvement leads to incremental thinking.
10x improvement forces you to rethink everything.

## How to Use

### Step 1: State your current metric
"Currently we [metric at X level]"

### Step 2: Imagine 10x
"What would it take to be at 10X level?"

### Step 3: Notice what breaks
At 10x, your current approach won't work. What has to change?

### Step 4: Work backward
If you HAD to do 10x, what would you do differently?

## Why It Works

**10% thinking:**
- Optimize current process
- Work harder
- Small tweaks
- Same fundamental approach

**10x thinking:**
- Question the entire approach
- Different leverage points
- Forces innovation
- New fundamental approach

You often can't get to 10x by doing more of the same.

## Example

**Goal:** Get more customers

**10% thinking:**
- Improve ads
- Hire another SDR
- Better landing page

**10x thinking:**
- What if customers came to us? (inbound/PLG)
- What if each customer brought 5 more? (virality)
- What if we sold to groups instead of individuals? (enterprise)
- What if we acquired a competitor?

Different questions. Different scale of solutions.


## Astro Teller's Version (Google X)

"It's often easier to make something 10 times better than it is to make it 10 percent better."

Why? Because 10% keeps you trapped in the current paradigm.
10x frees you to find entirely new approaches.

## When to Use

- Annual/quarterly planning
- When stuck optimizing incrementally
- When competition is closing in
- When the market is shifting
- When you want to think bigger

## The Reality Check

Not everything should be 10x'd. But thinking in 10x:
- Reveals the ceiling of your current approach
- Shows where breakthrough is possible
- Gets you out of incrementalism

Even if you end up at 3x, that beats incremental.
